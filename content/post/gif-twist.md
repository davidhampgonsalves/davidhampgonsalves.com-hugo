I saw a interesting timelapse animation on [Reddit](https://www.reddit.com/r/gifs/comments/4xdfa9/timescape_halls_harbour_nova_scotia/) and created [Gif-Twist](https://github.com/davidhampgonsalves/Gif-Twist) to automate the long manual processs (~10 hours).

Gif-Twist combines all the images of a timelapse into each output frame, displaying slices of each radiating around a central point.
